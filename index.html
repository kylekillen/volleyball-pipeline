Data Collection Methodology

This comprehensive database tracks the pathway from club volleyball to college men's volleyball programs across NCAA Division 1 and Division 2. It covers recruiting classes from 2021 through 2025, including all players currently on college rosters.

Data Sources

  • VolleyballMag Boys Fab 50 (2020-2024) - Annual ranking of top 50 high school seniors voted by D1-D2 coaches
  • JVA Boys All-National Team - Junior Volleyball Association's elite recognition across multiple class years
  • Official College Rosters - Current season rosters from 50+ college athletic websites
  • Player Bios & NCSA Profiles - Individual player pages with club/high school information
  • Tournament Results - AAU Nationals, USAV Junior Nationals, and other major events
  • Club Websites & SportsRecruits - Commitment announcements and historical player profiles

Divisions Covered

  • NCAA D1: UCLA, USC, Stanford, Pepperdine, BYU, Long Beach State, Ohio State, Penn State, Hawaii, and more
  • NCAA D2: Barton, Lees-McRae, King University (Conference Carolinas)

Recent National Champions

  • D1 2024: UCLA
  • D1 2025: Long Beach State

Data Limitations

This database represents publicly available information. Some players may have played for multiple club programs; the primary or most recent club affiliation is listed. International players who did not play US club volleyball are tracked separately and noted in the database but do not count toward US club rankings. Club affiliations marked as "Unknown" indicate players whose club history could not be verified.

Updates

Last updated: January 2026. Data reflects recruiting classes 2021-2025 across D1 and D2 programs with comprehensive research from official rosters, recruiting profiles, and tournament data.
